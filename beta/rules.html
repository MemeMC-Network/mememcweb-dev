Server Rules

Please follow these rules to ensure everyone has a great experience

1

No Griefing

Do not destroy or modify other players' builds without permission. This includes stealing items from chests in survival mode.

2

No Cheating

Use of hacked clients, exploits, or any unfair advantages is strictly prohibited. This includes x-ray texture packs and duping glitches.

3

Be Respectful

Treat all players with respect. No harassment, hate speech, discrimination, or inappropriate behavior will be tolerated.

4

No Spamming

Avoid excessive messages in chat, command flooding, or advertising other servers. Keep chat clean and enjoyable for everyone.

5

Follow Staff

Always comply with staff instructions and decisions. If you disagree with a decision, discuss it respectfully in private messages.

6

No NSFW Content

Keep all content appropriate for all ages. No sexual, violent, or otherwise inappropriate builds, skins, or discussions.

Breaking these rules may result in warnings, temporary bans, or permanent bans depending on severity. Our staff team reserves the right to take appropriate action.
